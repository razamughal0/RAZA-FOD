    • About Us

    RAZA FOOD, was founded by m.Raza Mughal in 2000 and has since become one of the largest fast-food chains in the world. Known for its signature fried chicken recipe with 11 secret herbs and spices, KFC has provided delicious meals to millions across the globe.

    Our Commitment to Quality

    At RAZA FOOD, we are committed to serving the highest quality food. Our chickens are always fresh, never frozen, and are prepared using only the finest ingredients. Each meal is made with care and is crafted to provide a satisfying and memorable dining experience.

    Global Reach, Local Touch

    With over 24,000 locations worldwide, we serve diverse cultures and cater to local tastes. Whether it’s our crispy fried chicken, tender chicken tenders, or other regional specialties, RAZA FOOD global presence ensures that great food is always within reach.

    Our Mission & Vision

    Our mission is simple: to provide delicious, high-quality food that everyone can enjoy. KFC strives to be the leader in the quick-service restaurant industry by staying true to our core values: quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We aim to expand our brand while maintaining the same level of excellence that our customers have come to know and love.
